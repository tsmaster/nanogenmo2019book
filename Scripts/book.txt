Chapter 1 : Adrionnu Harxer
---

Adrionnu Harxer was a male drow that lived in Londun. Londun was
a nice town. Nondescript, for now. Adrionnu had a mentor, named
Quagan Migdezu. Also nondescript. Quagan tells Adrionnu 'I call
you to adventure!'. But Adrionnu says 'No sir! I refuse the call
to adventure. I want to stay here in Londun and smoke pipes at
Tosci's Power Converters and Pipe shop'.
