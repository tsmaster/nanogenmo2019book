Chapter 1 : Wajqey Xhaniw
---

Wajqey Xhaniw was a female human that lived in Skaxa krie. Skaxa
krie was a nice town. Nondescript, for now. Wajqey had a mentor,
named Esajelle Milmon. Also nondescript. Esajelle tells Wajqey
'I call you to adventure!'. But Wajqey says 'No ma'am! I refuse
the call to adventure. I want to stay here in Skaxa krie and
smoke pipes at Tosci's Power Converters and Pipe shop'.

Chapter 3 : The feast of the harpy
---

And hast thou killed the harpy, my daughter? Calloo! Callay! We
must have a feast!

((antipasto)).

The fifth course was entree. It consisted of jellied shrimp,
topped with Mushroom sauce.

cabbage, and looseleaf lettuce topped with sesame seeds and
wonton strips.

The eighth course was soup. It consisted of Pea soup.

whale, stuffed inside a pressed ram.

The tenth course was salad. It consisted of frisée tossed with
tatsoi topped with sunflower seeds and sesame seeds.

The eleventh course was soup. It consisted of Caldillo de
congrio.

The twelfth course was entree. It consisted of grilled unicorn.

green bell pepper strips, cucumber sticks, and cassava.

The fourteenth course was hors d'oeuvre. It consisted of bread
sticks and cheddar cheese rangoon.

The final course was dessert. It consisted of apple pie.
