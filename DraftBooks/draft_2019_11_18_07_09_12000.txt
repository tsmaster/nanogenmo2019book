Chapter 1 : Iaren Woimer
---

Iaren Woimer was a female halfling that lived in Qkaru bhau.
Qkaru bhau was a nice town. Nondescript, for now. Iaren had a
mentor, named Riin Fergamnez. Also nondescript. Riin tells Iaren
'I call you to adventure!'. But Iaren says 'No ma'am! I refuse
the call to adventure. I want to stay here in Qkaru bhau and
smoke pipes at Tosci's Power Converters and Pipe shop'.
