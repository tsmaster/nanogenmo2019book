Chapter 1 : Kamey Nichokp
---

Kamey Nichokp was a male half-elf that lived in Uimhi. Uimhi was
a nice town. Nondescript, for now. Kamey had a mentor, named
Chrilkian Gahx. Also nondescript. Chrilkian tells Kamey 'I call
you to adventure!'. But Kamey says 'No sir! I refuse the call to
adventure. I want to stay here in Uimhi and smoke pipes at
Tosci's Power Converters and Pipe shop'.
