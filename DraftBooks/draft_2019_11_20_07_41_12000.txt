Chapter 1 : Entonau Jaljy
---

Entonau Jaljy was a male dwarf that lived in Shurkroosa. Entonau
was juvenile and bold. Shurkroosa was a nice town. Nondescript,
for now. Entonau had a mentor, named Karowe Snydev. Karowe was
along in years and sharp. Karowe told Entonau 'I call you to
adventure!'. But Entonau said 'No ma'am! I refuse the call to
adventure. I want to stay here in Shurkroosa and smoke pipes at
Tosci's Power Converters and Pipe shop'.
