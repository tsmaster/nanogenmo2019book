Chapter 1 : Nichulos Hulziamw
---

Nichulos Hulziamw was a male halfling that lived in Sefbl. Sefbl
was a nice town. Nondescript, for now. Nichulos had a mentor,
named Tumotlw Hoped. Also nondescript. Tumotlw tells Nichulos 'I
call you to adventure!'. But Nichulos says 'No ma'am! I refuse
the call to adventure. I want to stay here in Sefbl and smoke
pipes at Tosci's Power Converters and Pipe shop'.
