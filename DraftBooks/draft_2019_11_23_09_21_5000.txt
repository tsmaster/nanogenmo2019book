Chapter 1 : Ale-chrustien Båfdsot
---

Ale-chrustien Båfdsot was a female elf that lived in Longtuwn.
Ale-chrustien was childish and ignorant. Longtuwn was a nice
town. Longtuwn was little and whimsical. Ale-chrustien had a
mentor, named Loav Palondur. Loav was gray and perceptive.

Loav told Ale-chrustien 'I call you to adventure!'.

But Ale-chrustien said 'No, ma'am! I refuse the call to
adventure. I want to stay here in Longtuwn and sing songs under
the bridge'.
