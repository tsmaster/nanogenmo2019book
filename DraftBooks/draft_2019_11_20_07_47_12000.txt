Chapter 1 : Urouna Wizw
---

Urouna Wizw was a female half-elf that lived in Stivenaju.
Urouna was childlike and unseasoned. Stivenaju was a nice town.
Stivenaju was modest and unusual. Urouna had a mentor, named
Wujliam Mopop. Wujliam was decrepit and shrewd. Wujliam told
Urouna 'I call you to adventure!'. But Urouna said 'No ma'am! I
refuse the call to adventure. I want to stay here in Stivenaju
and smoke pipes at Tosci's Power Converters and Pipe shop'.
