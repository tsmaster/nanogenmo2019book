Chapter 1 : Qemes Olriq
---

Qemes Olriq was a male drow that lived in Wallasey. Qemes was
raw and brash. Wallasey was a nice town. Wallasey was diminutive
and weird. Qemes had a mentor, named Lionaxde Gon. Lionaxde was
gray and shrewd.

Lionaxde told Qemes 'I call you to adventure!'.

But Qemes said 'No, sir! I refuse the call to adventure. I want
to stay here in Wallasey and read stories under the bridge'.
