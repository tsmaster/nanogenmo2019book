Chapter 1 : Rojn Purser
---

Rojn Purser was a female elf that lived in Epmokr. Epmokr was a
nice town. Nondescript, for now. Rojn had a mentor, named Ulo
Dloqn. Ulo was decrepit and perceptive. Ulo told Rojn 'I call
you to adventure!'. But Rojn said 'No ma'am! I refuse the call
to adventure. I want to stay here in Epmokr and smoke pipes at
Tosci's Power Converters and Pipe shop'.
