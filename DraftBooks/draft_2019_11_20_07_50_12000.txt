Chapter 1 : Demavan Groy
---

town. Nurth Vanwoivet was small and curious. Demavan had a
mentor, named Fuith Adazr. Fuith was decrepit and educated.

'No respected one! I refuse the call to adventure. I want to
stay here in Nurth Vanwoivet and smoke pipes at Tosci's Power
Converters and Pipe shop'.
