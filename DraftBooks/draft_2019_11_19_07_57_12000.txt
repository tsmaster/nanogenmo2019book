Chapter 1 : Edtin Veervo
---

Edtin Veervo was a female half-human that lived in Bisipdub.
Bisipdub was a nice town. Nondescript, for now. Edtin had a
mentor, named Yauy Gozfon. Also nondescript. Yauy tells Edtin 'I
call you to adventure!'. But Edtin says 'No ma'am! I refuse the
call to adventure. I want to stay here in Bisipdub and smoke
pipes at Tosci's Power Converters and Pipe shop'.
