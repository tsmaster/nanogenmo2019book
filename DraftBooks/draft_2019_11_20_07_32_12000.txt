Chapter 1 : Tiasna Ramured
---

Tiasna Ramured was a male half-halfling that lived in Anlorya La
Vecla. Anlorya La Vecla was a nice town. Nondescript, for now.
Tiasna had a mentor, named Alejenfro Buplom. Alejenfro was
decrepit and sensible. Alejenfro told Tiasna 'I call you to
adventure!'. But Tiasna said 'No sir! I refuse the call to
adventure. I want to stay here in Anlorya La Vecla and smoke
pipes at Tosci's Power Converters and Pipe shop'.
